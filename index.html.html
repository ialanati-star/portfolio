Project 04
Suspension Pickup Bracket
SolidWorks CNC Machining Suspension Geometry Adjustable Design
CNC-machined suspension pickup bracket designed for adjustable suspension geometry on the FSAE vehicle. The C-channel cross-section provides high bending stiffness with low mass, while slotted adjustment features allow in-field ride height and alignment tuning within a 0.95-inch envelope without component replacement. Load-bearing wall thickness sized for worst-case suspension load, with slot tolerances permitting adjustment while maintaining positional repeatability. Designed as a single CNC part from billet aluminum.
